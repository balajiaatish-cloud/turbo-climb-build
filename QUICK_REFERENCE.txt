  Find: 0xff3333
  Change to: 0x0000ff (blue), 0x00ff00 (green), etc.

  File: js/gameconfig.js
  Increase: maxFuel, maxHealth

Increase Obstacles:
  File: js/scenes/gamescene.js  
  Find: Math.random() < 0.2
  Change: 0.2 to higher number

Boosters:       Restore health and fuel instantly
Health:         Red bar, regenerates slowly
Fuel:           Green bar, regenerates, used for boost
Distance:       How high you've climbed (meters)
